Built in Canada

A Canadian company, aligned with AI for All.

On June 4, 2026, Canada launched AI for All — a national AI strategy built on three pillars: building trust, creating opportunity, and reinforcing sovereignty. Go!Gosling is a Canadian company built on those same principles, and you can see them in how the app works.

Building trust

AI you can use with confidence

Your chats and Apple Health data are processed on your iPhone. Health never leaves the device, no account is needed to start, and anything that books, sends, or pays asks first.

Reinforcing sovereignty

Your data, on your terms

We are a Canadian company. Your most personal data stays on your device — under your control, not on someone else’s servers.

Creating opportunity

AI that is genuinely for all

No sign-up, no jargon — just say what you need. We are building a capable assistant that anyone can open and use.
