How it works

Three steps. That's it.

One conversation on your iPhone — Gosli routes chat, health, and maps, shows you the plan, and asks before it acts. No account needed to start.

1

Get it on iPhone

One tap from the App Store at launch. Open Gosli and start talking — chat and health work without signing in.

2

Tell Gosli what you want

Speak or type in plain language. “How did I sleep?” routes to Health on your iPhone. “Draft a kind reply” stays in Chat on-device. “Find a café and open Maps” may use our server — never your health data.

3

See the plan, tap Allow once

Before anything books, sends, pays, or shares, Gosli shows what it will do. You tap Allow once — every time. Memory saves only when you say yes.
