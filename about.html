About us

A small Ontario team, building private AI.

Go!Gosling is an early-stage AI company in Ontario, Canada. We're building a private, on-device assistant for iPhone — one that turns what you say into action, and keeps your life on your device.

Why we exist

Powerful AI shouldn't cost you your privacy.

AI is racing ahead, and too often the deal is: hand over your data to get the magic. We don't accept that trade. Go!Gosling does the smart part on your iPhone, asks before it acts, and never makes your private life the product. We're starting small and focused — Chat, Health, and Maps — and growing the same way: on your terms.

What we believe

On-device by default

Your health and your conversations belong on your phone — not on someone's server.

Asks before it acts

An agent that takes action must earn trust — so it always asks first, every time.

AI for all

No jargon, no sign-up wall. A capable assistant anyone can just open and use.

Built in Canada

Proudly Ontario-based, aligned with Canada's AI for All — trust, opportunity, sovereignty.
